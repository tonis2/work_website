Mobile banking & wallet app

Paysure — fintech, EU · Freelance engagement · 2023–ongoing (3 years)

At a glance Freelance engagement for Paysure, an EU fintech. We led the app-side development of its mobile banking product — a Flutter app spanning onboarding, card management, wallet provisioning and payments on iOS and Android.
FlutterWallet / Push provisioningSecure architecture

The problem. Paysure needed to put a full banking experience in its customers' hands — issuing payment cards, adding them straight to Apple Pay and Google Pay, and moving money — all inside one app on both iOS and Android. In fintech there is no room for rough edges or security gaps, so the app had to clear a high bar for both user experience and protection of sensitive financial data.

The approach. We built the app in Flutter so iOS and Android shipped from a single codebase without giving up native capabilities. Payment cards were added to the device wallets through in-app push provisioning, taking users from opening the app to tap-to-pay in a few steps. We then took the app through the full App Store and Play review and release process for a regulated financial product.

The outcome. The app shipped to the App Store and Google Play and now serves tens of thousands of users, putting card issuing, wallet provisioning and payments in their hands from one Flutter codebase. The engagement has run since 2023 and is ongoing — a long-term relationship built on a product the client is happy with.
